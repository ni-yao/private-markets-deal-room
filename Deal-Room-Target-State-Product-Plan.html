01Executive summary

Private‑markets deal teams run on disconnected point tools — data re‑keyed across PitchBook, Preqin, Excel and CRM; subjective prioritization; siloed CIM review; hand‑stitched IC memos; after‑the‑fact compliance. Specialist MDs are pulled in late and sequentially, so risk surfaces after time and money are committed. The Deal Room replaces this with one governed deal record and governed AI agents across the flow — every figure grounded and cited to source.

02From current state to target state

Today the deal breaks down at every stage. The target state connects origination → screening → diligence → IC in one intelligent workflow.

Current state — breaks down at every stage

Stage 1 · Origination & Screening
  • Sourcing is network‑bound & manual — weeks from signal to conviction
  • Data re‑keyed across PitchBook / Preqin / Excel / CRM — no single source of truth
  • No AI scoring — prioritization is subjective & inconsistent
  • AI & Supply‑Chain MDs engaged late — risk found after time is sunk
Stage 2 · Diligence & Approval
  • CIMs, financials & legal reviewed in silos; manual PDF extraction — weeks of effort
  • Four workstreams disconnected — cross‑domain risk slips through
  • IC memo stitched by hand — inconsistent numbers, version chaos
  • No shared audit trail; after‑the‑fact SFDR / ILPA compliance

Target state — one governed record, agents across the flow

  • Continuous AI sourcing — a ranked, persona‑tagged feed surfaced in Teams/Copilot
  • One governed deal estate in Fabric — no re‑keying, one source of truth
  • AI scoring + strategic‑fit ranking at triage
  • Sector / AI / Supply‑chain MDs shift‑left — captured at triage, not later
  • Document Intelligence parses CIMs in minutes → Fabric → LBO model
  • Connected diligence lanes write to one shared record — cross‑risk surfaces
  • IC memo drafts itself from the live record — consistent, cited numbers
  • SFDR / ILPA checks & full audit trail in‑flow (Purview)
Root cause: No unified, AI‑driven deal flow connects origination → screening → diligence → IC in one intelligent workflow. The shared record is the unlock — a number the Supply‑Chain MD validates is instantly visible in the AI MD's plan.

04Product design

Seven capability modules mapped to the deal lifecycle, powered by a roster of governed Foundry agents, all writing to one governed deal record.

Design principles

① One governed record

A single source of truth from signal to monitoring — no re‑keying.

② Grounded & cited

Every agent figure traces to a source document or dataset (RAG + citations).

③ Agents draft, humans decide

Agents scan, parse and draft; MDs review and approve at gates.

④ Shift‑left expertise

Sector / AI / ops views captured at triage, not after the fact.

⑤ Compliance in‑flow

SFDR / ILPA checks & audit trail embedded in the workflow (Purview).

⑥ Work where teams work

Teams, Excel, Word, SharePoint, Copilot — no new island UI.

The governed deal record (core concept)

A logical entity that physically spans Dynamics 365 / Dataverse (pipeline, decisions, approvals), Microsoft Fabric / OneLake (analytical deal estate), the SharePoint data room (documents + sensitivity labels), and Microsoft Purview (lineage & audit). Agents read/write through governed tools so the same validated number appears consistently to every persona and in every artifact.

08Security, compliance & governance (FSI)

Built for a regulated industry: EU data residency, in‑flow compliance, governed agent identities, private networking and responsible‑AI guardrails.

Data residency

All AI + data in Sweden Central; Azure Policy enforces region, private endpoints & CMK.

Compliance in‑flow

Microsoft Cloud for FS baseline + Purview: lineage, audit trail, sensitivity labels, DLP and SFDR / ILPA evidence generated as the memo assembles — not after the fact.

Identity

Entra ID + Conditional Access + PIM; Entra Agent ID per agent; least‑privilege RBAC; managed identities (no secrets in code).

Network isolation

VNet + Private Endpoints for OpenAI, Search, Storage, Cosmos, Key Vault; no public data plane.

Responsible AI

Content Safety + prompt shields; groundedness + citations on every output; Foundry evaluations; human‑in‑the‑loop at O4/D4.

Auditability

Immutable audit trail across Dataverse, SharePoint, OneLake — unified in Purview for a fully compliant, auditable pattern.
